How to fill the electron "bunk beds":

Electrons are negatively charged and repel each other, like magnets. To share a space, they need to be distinct.

  • First Click (Up Arrow): Places the first electron. Electrons are like siblings who want their own rooms—fill every empty box in a row with one "up" electron before pairing them up.
  • Second Click (Down Arrow): Places a second electron. To share the room without "fighting" (repelling), it must spin the opposite way.
  • Third Click: Clears the box.

Scientific Terms: Filling low energy first = Aufbau Principle. Giving everyone their own room before pairing = Hund's Rule.
