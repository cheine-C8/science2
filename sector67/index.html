Fundamental Principles

Sector 4: Newton's Three Laws of Motion

Key Card: 4
🎯 Mission Checkpoint: Let's summarize the rules of physics!
  • 1st Law (Inertia): Objects keep doing what they are doing unless a net force acts.
  • 2nd Law ($F = m a$): Force equals mass times acceleration.
  • 3rd Law (Action/Reaction): Every force has an equal and opposite reaction force.
