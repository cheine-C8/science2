Fidelity & Accuracy Tips

1 Ensure 2D Linear Motion

For maximum accuracy, objects must move left-to-right (or vice versa) perpendicular to the camera frame. Objects moving towards or away from the camera violate perspective ratios and report skewed tracking values.

2 Scale Calibration is Critical

The webcam is 2D. You must drag the custom calibration golden handles over an item in the video frame whose length you know exactly (like a 30cm ruler, standard paper sheet, etc.). The closer your tracked object is to this reference distance, the more exact your measurements are.

3 Optimize Lighting & Shadows

In Speed Gates mode, the app monitors sudden spikes in pixel color brightness/depth along the lines. Glare, moving camera shadows, or shaky camera positioning can cause false positive gate trips.

4 Avoid Frame Blurring

Webcams automatically lengthen exposure times in dark rooms, creating "ghosting" or motion blur. Perform tracking in bright rooms to keep camera shutter speed high and blur low.
