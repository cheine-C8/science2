Lab Protocol

1. Plug the spectrometer into your computer.
2. Search for a program called "Spectrometry" and open it.
3. Note: All cuvettes must be placed with the cap up and the flat sides to the left and right.
4. Calibrate Dark by placing your hand over the opening and running calibration.
5. Calibrate Reference by placing the cuvette with water into the spectrometer and running calibration.
6. Place a sample in the spectrometer and record the absorbance.
7. Take a screenshot (Save Data) and repeat for each sample.

Follow the highlighted step to proceed.
